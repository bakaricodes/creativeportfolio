My story...

It started in Baltimore in the 80's and has included lots of adventurous twists and turns. Keep reading to learn more about how it continues to unfold.

As a front-end web developer, event, and idea producer I combine design and style elements, mixed media, technology, and amazing food (whenever possible) to create unique DIGITAL and PHYSICAL spaces for people to learn, connect, and celebrate with each other.

Thus far, my curiosities have blessed me with opportunities to travel throughout the country and build a network of brilliant creatives. So it's only fitting that my next venture honors the courage & creativity of my 7-year-old self.
